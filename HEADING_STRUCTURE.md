## Key Features

### Heading Characteristics
- ✅ **Responsive Sizing**: `text-2xl sm:text-3xl md:text-4xl lg:text-5xl`
- ✅ **Bold Weight**: `font-bold`
- ✅ **Dark Color**: `text-slate-900`
- ✅ **Proper Spacing**: `mb-4` or `mb-6`

### Subheading Characteristics
- ✅ **Responsive Sizing**: `text-sm sm:text-base md:text-lg`
- ✅ **Medium Weight**: `font-medium` or `font-semibold`
- ✅ **Gray Color**: `text-slate-600`
- ✅ **Max Width**: `max-w-2xl` for readability

### Animation
- ✅ **Entrance Animation**: `animate-fade-in-up`
- ✅ **Staggered Delays**: `animationDelay: '0.1s'`, `'0.2s'`, etc.
- ✅ **Duration**: 0.6s ease-out
